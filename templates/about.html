What is Customer Churn Prediction?

Customer churn prediction is the process of identifying which customers are likely to stop doing business with a company. By analyzing customer behavior and historical data, businesses can proactively address issues before customers decide to leave.

Instead of reacting to customer loss after it happens, churn prediction enables companies to take preventive measures, improving customer satisfaction and retention rates.

Common Churn Indicators

Behavioral Indicators

  • Decreased product usage
  • Reduced login frequency
  • Fewer support interactions
  • Declining satisfaction scores

Business Indicators

  • Payment issues or declines
  • Contract renewal approaching
  • Competitor offerings in market
  • Recent price changes
